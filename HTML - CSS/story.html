Their story

Everybody loves eating pastas, burgers, fries, sandwiches and other good things with some sauce.

It's also the case of the Parisians ! These ones, very well raised, sometimes leave rests of their meals in the street wich make the enjoyment of the little stomachs of small pigeons and gulls (yes, there are gulls in Paris).

But a day... A new sauce the ©Espicylly Hot Sauce is created. Everybody is crazy about it. We put it in all the meals. The Parisians, very generous people, continue to leave their rests on the curbs, in the overflowing trash cans, on the benches of parks...

But there is a problem. This sauce is a disaster for our dears little birds ! Not only it is not in their taste, but moreover, it gives them horribly stomachaches!

After an extreme emergency meeting, they developped a devillish plan. Why not to divert this harmful weapon against the Parisians ? They already feed them less and less, they run after them, the throw pebbles and treat them as pest ! It's the last stram that breaks the camel's back !


Arm yourself cause...

The war is declared ! Let's drop espicylly explosive bombs !
