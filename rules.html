The rules are in the main page too but... Anyway ~

Your goal is to take revenge of the Parisians who bullied you :

The Simple Parisian : 1 box

The Parisian and her stroller : 2 boxes

The Parisian's car : 3 boxes

The class of little Parisians : 4 boxes

The Tourists : 5 boxes

In order to restore justice you have to follow these instructions :

Choose a box on the gameboard.

If behind the box a Parisian is hiding, you can play a second time.

If not, you lose a try.

Good luck little bird! :D
